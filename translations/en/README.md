# üöÄ Cybersecurity for Beginners ‚Äì a curriculum  

In this fast-paced era of AI adoption, understanding how to secure IT systems is more important than ever. This course is designed to teach you the basics of cybersecurity, helping you start your journey in security. It is vendor-neutral and divided into short lessons that take approximately 30-60 minutes to complete. Each lesson includes a brief quiz and links to additional resources for deeper exploration of the topic.
